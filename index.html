Pen of the Week

MatryoshBox

Amit Sheen

This pen is an infinite loop of boxes jumping out of each other. The creater used nth-child to change the color of the different boxes. He used some animation to make the boxes "bounce" as he calls it and to open them up. He used grid as the display with a radial gradiant to make the ends of the platform rounded.

See the Pen MatryoshBox (CSS) by Amit Sheen (@amit_sheen) on CodePen.
